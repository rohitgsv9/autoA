 Add Telegrambot

If you want to share your messages with your telegram followers it's very easy and so fast than before. This is only possible if you can add a bot in that channel as admin or you are a admin who can add bot and give post permission.

Search BotFather official acount

Send /newbot to Botfather

Send your Botname


Send username and get Token

Open Telegram Channel/Group

Click on Administrator

Click on Add Admin

Search and Find your bot

Give Permission to bot and save it
