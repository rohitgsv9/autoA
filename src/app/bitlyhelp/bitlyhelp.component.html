Bitly Token Process

Sign up bitly here  Sign Up

1. Make new account of use your existing account go and login in your account Links Bitly
2. Go to menu that you will find in top right corner of bitly dashboard
3. Than you will be able to see your name and Email just click on it means click on your profile.
4. Than you will be able to see a menu "Generic Access Token" just click on it.
5. Than you will be able to see Password Field just enter your password in it and you will see a button To Generate Token Just press it and copy your token.
Note : If you signed up with Google or Social media you need to set your password first. You can try by using forget password too.
